Salmon temaki

How to make a Salmon temaki

The Salmon temaki became very popular in western countries. A more compact version of the traditional japanese sushi

This guide contains the Instructions on how to make the Salmon temaki and the ingredients needed.

Ingridients

  • Smoked salmon, slices 4 pcs
  • Sushi rice, cooked 1 cup
  • Rice vinegar 1 tsp
  • Nori dried seaweed wraps 2 sheets
  • Cucumber, sliced into strips 4 strips
  • Avocado, sliced into strips 1 strips
  • Mayonnaise As needed

Steps

  1. Combine the cooked rice with vinegar. Set aside to let cool.
  2. Slice the cucumber and avocado into thick, pencil-like strips.
  3. Cut the nori sheets into half and place the shiny side down.
  4. With damp hands, shape a handful of rice (about Â¼ cup) into a cone shape
  5. Compress rice slightly on one side of the cone with your thumb. Spread some wasabi and mayonnaise into the indentation and pack it with a slice of salmon and strips of cucumber and avocado.
  6. Place the rice cone diagonally on one side of the nori sheet, with the sharp edge of the cone pointing to the center of the bottom edge.
  7. Take the closer corner of the nori and fold over rice cone.
  8. Keeping the nori tight, continue to roll it over the rice cone using the pointed end of the rice cone as the central point.
  9. Seal edge with a little water.
